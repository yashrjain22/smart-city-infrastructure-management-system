The Smart City Infrastructure Management System is a centralized digital platform that enables citizens to report civic infrastructure issues and track their resolution in a transparent, efficient, and accountable manner.

About the Portal

Urban local bodies require robust digital systems to manage public infrastructure and civic services efficiently. This portal provides a unified mechanism for grievance registration, monitoring, and redressal across urban governance departments.

The system is designed in alignment with national digital governance standards and supports improved decision-making through structured complaint data.

Key Features

Location-Based Reporting

Automatic detection of complaint location using standard geolocation services.

Unique Complaint ID

Every grievance is assigned a unique reference number for transparent tracking.

Status Monitoring

Citizens can track complaint progress: Pending, In Progress, or Resolved.

Administrative Oversight

Authorities can manage, update, and resolve complaints efficiently.

Services Covered

  • Roads, footpaths, and potholes
  • Water supply and drainage systems
  • Electricity and street lighting
  • Solid waste management and sanitation
  • Public utility and municipal services
